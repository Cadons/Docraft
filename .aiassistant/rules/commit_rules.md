Commit message rules for this repo:

1. Use the format: `<type>: <subject>`.
    - Types: feat, fix, docs, refactor, test, chore, build, ci, perf.
2. Subject is imperative, present tense; no trailing period; <= 72 chars.
3. Add a blank line before the body when needed.
4. Body explains why and what changed; wrap at 72 chars.
5. Reference issues or tickets at the end, e.g. `Refs #123` or `Fixes #123`.
6. Avoid WIP, "temp", or "misc" in commit messages.
